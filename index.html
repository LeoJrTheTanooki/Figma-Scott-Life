May 2004 Life Update

It's certainly been some time since I've updated here, hasn't it? Quite frankly I was in a really bad spot for a good couple of months... my girlfriend disappeared on me (literally, she shined brightly and then vanished in front of me), I got locked out of her apartment, and worst of all her cat ran away, so you can guess what all that combined can do to a guy's psyche. Not to mention all that fighting evil exes thing for her love was for nothing... or so I thought, but I'll get to that soon, right now I want to focus what I had gotten up to during that time.

I had been doing... nothing at all throughout that period except grieving through self destructive ways. Eventually my gay friend who built this website for me suggested that I go out again to try and get over my disappeared girlfriend, and the first girl I see when he suggested that... was Knives, the highschooler that I dated last year to get over my previous ex, who had turned 18 recently. Considering my mindset by then, and the fact that she was 18 now, you can kinda guess how well I handled the conversation with her during that time (very horribly). We're still friends and found some closure though so I suppose it wasn't so bad. And then I ran into Envy…

Her own blog was the reason I started this website in the first place, what with Knives constantly checking hers, I had to have my own to seem cool with her (thanks again Wallace). I was told by another friend to insult someone to bring them down, and of course my jackass brain picked Envy “Famous and Adored Singer” Adams in person… in front of a crowd no less… We met up somewhere else a bit later and had a more civil discussion and some closure as well, so there's no more beef between us anymore. Ramona's cat also came back, and believe me I took care of him as best as I could the second he did.

Ultimately I went on a wilderness sabbatical in Northern Ontario to think about some junk and met up with Kim, the drummer of our band who moved away… and an ex of mine. We went on the sabbatical together and I uh... kissed her... (look I was really desperate, okay) and though she initially did it back, she pushed me away out of rejection and confronted me of my memories back in high school as I fought my Negative counterpart. So you know how I talked about my time in high school a while back? Fighting a school and saying goodbye to Kim the first time around? Yeah turns out I remembered all that wrong and now I feel horrible, though it was necessary to remember everything to prevent myself form making those dumb mistakes again. I also relented to the Nega-Scott and he became a part of me once again. Turns out he was the manifestation of me running from my mistakes and our reunion led to my memories coming back, warts and all.

Long story short, I went back to Toronto, confronted Gideon Graves, he monologues about calling the evil exes through craigslist, we fought for a bit, I got killed and revived thanks to an extra life I had, Ramona came out of my chest and we fought Gideon together. Oh I also went into Ramona's mind through her subspace bag because Gideon was messing with her head through there and we finally finished him off. If he respawns I hope he goes to jail because this guy literally kidnapped and froze six of his exes with Ramona being the last one he wanted, not exactly someone you want roaming around the streets. Anyhow we're all doing a lot better now.

I should mention that Ramona left to her dad's and also went on a sabbatical retreat when she disappeared on me, intending on improving herself, but she kinda just stayed inside all day anyways, like how I did when she left. Man looking back at this website is already filling me with so much regret, but then again I suppose that's what growth is. I'm still unsure if we'll continue this relationship, but I feel like there's a good chance we will. And if we do, I'll avoid making the same mistakes with Ramona this time around. We both screwed up, but that doesn't mean we can't grow. Anyways I still have to figure some of my own junk out for real this time. For myself this time, not just for Ramona.

~SP
